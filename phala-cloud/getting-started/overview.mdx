Phala Cloud provides secure, privacy-preserving application hosting through TEE technology. This guide will walk you through the essential steps to get started with Phala Cloud and deploy your first CVM in just a few minutes.

## Getting Started

What you'll accomplish:
- Establish a secure Phala Cloud account
- Deploy your first confidential computing environment
- Experience TEE-powered privacy protection
- Access enterprise-grade security features

Follow these steps to set up your Phala Cloud application:

<Steps>
<Step>
### [Create Your Phala Cloud Account](/phala-cloud/getting-started/sign-up-for-cloud-account) (1 minute)

Begin by creating your official Phala Cloud account. Visit our [registration page](https://cloud.phala.network/register) to establish your secure cloud environment and gain access to our TEE-powered infrastructure.
</Step>

<Step>
### [Deploy Your First CVM](/phala-cloud/getting-started/start-from-cloud-ui) (2-3 minutes)

After account creation, deploy your inaugural CVM. Start with a pre-configured Jupyter Notebook environment to experience the power of confidential computing without writing any code.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Note type="info">
**Alternative Deployment Method**: For users who prefer command-line interfaces, we offer a comprehensive CLI solution. Learn more about [deploying via Cloud CLI](/phala-cloud/phala-cloud-cli/start-from-cloud-cli).
</Note>
